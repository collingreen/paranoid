Stupid Sites

Turn on 'Stupid Site Mode' if the site you are using limits the length of passwords they accept.

You should strongly consider emailing the site owners and requesting that they allow unlimited length passwords. Sites that constrain password lengths are being irresponsible with your data and you should demand that they take security more seriously.

Premium Paranoid can remember this setting for you for all the different sites you use, making dealing with these stupid sites a little bit easier.
